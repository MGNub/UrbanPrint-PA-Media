Why We Made This Website

A website about walkable cities aims to promote urban designs that prioritize pedestrians, fostering healthier, sustainable, and vibrant communities. It educates on walkability’s benefits—lower emissions, better health, and stronger economies—while sharing expert insights and innovative planning solutions. By inspiring planners, policymakers, and citizens, it advocates for safe, accessible infrastructure, driving change toward more livable cities.
